# lists

Lightweight queues and histories only. Keep these files easy to scan.

- `reading.md`
  - books in progress go under `## currently reading`
  - finished books go under `## read`, grouped by the year read
  - insert finished books into the right `### YYYY` section; create the year if needed
  - unread books go under `## to read`
  - keep the current entry style: bold title, italic author, rating only when known

- `watch.md`
  - watched items go under `## binged`
  - split by `### tv` and `### movies`
  - order binged entries by release year, new to old, not by watch date
  - unwatched items go under `## to watch`, again split into `tv` and `movies`

- do not add extra metadata or commentary
- keep headings and capitalization consistent with the file
