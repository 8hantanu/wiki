# stung treng

## laos and cambodia

### 2025-04-03

I leave [4000 islands](../4000-islands#2025-04-02) early in the morning.

#### 0630

I wake up, pack, and get ready to cross into Cambodia.

#### 0800

I go for breakfast, an iced cappuccino and some food, and the hostel drops us at the pier.

#### 0900

I realise our boat pickup is actually at a different spot. While figuring it out, I find a Laotian janosa stall and have one stuffed with pork, pumpkin, herbs, and a mango samosa.

#### 1030

A buggy takes us to the bus.

#### midday

At the border, the bus host warns us about some "questioning." An officer asks random questions and demands proof of cash, saying we need $500 to proceed.

We have about $509 in total, explain ourselves, and then wait around for half an hour. Good thing we do not end up paying any penalty.

#### after

Back on the bus, only our bags get drilled for documents. If it were not for our host Jovan, I do not think we would have made it out of Laos without paying a bribe.

#### 1130

We get moving again, eat a good plate of fried rice and tofu with coffee, clear immigration, and continue onward through Cambodia.
