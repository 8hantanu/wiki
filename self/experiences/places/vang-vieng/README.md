# vang vieng

## laos and cambodia

### 2025-03-29

I arrive from [Luang Prabang](../luang-prabang#2025-03-29) and take a minibus to the hostel. We rent a scooty nearby.

#### afternoon

Lunch is fried rice and a pork dish at a cafe with a nice view of the hills.

#### 1600

We head to Tham Chang Cave. The place is huge, and I walk about half a kilometer inside. At first I am scared, but the white cave is beautiful and I settle into it.

A man at the security booth offers us a ride, which turns out to be a lot of fun. I also take a dip into the water from around 28 m.

#### evening

It is still too bright for sunset, so we talk about doing a viewpoint but never actually make it there.

#### 1900

We head to the night market. I have a slice of pizza, beer, and fried rice, and buy some Laotian coffee while wandering around.

#### 2100

Back at the hostel, we book paragliding for the next day, chill by the pool with drinks, and doze off around 2300.

---

### 2025-03-31

#### 0600

I wake up to the sight of hot air balloons taking off over Vang Vieng.

#### morning

Our tuk-tuk picks us up for paragliding. I expect to puke but end up loving it. The flight lasts around 15-20 minutes and gives me huge views across the whole valley.

#### 0800

I get back to the hostel for breakfast: bread, fruit, and a nice spread.

#### 0930

We head to Blue Lagoon 3, stopping for coconut water on the way.

The lagoon is huge and about 4 m deep. Once I get over the hesitation, I jump in properly. We zip-line, cliff-dive, paddle, and explore around.

#### afternoon

We try to find the nearby cave entrance and fail, but meet Vinny, who is collecting trash to keep the area clean. We join him for a while and end up exploring the area better because of him.

#### late afternoon

We decide on one more flight, then spring rolls, and later make our way to Blue Lagoon 1 to relax.
