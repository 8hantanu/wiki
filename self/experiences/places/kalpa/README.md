# kalpa

## the spiti-kinnaur trail

### 2024-10-18

The road to Kalpa from [kaza](../kaza#2024-10-18) is filled with beautiful views. All the way, all I can see are trucks and tempos filled with boxes of apples.

At 1700, we reach Zostel Kalpa. The rooms at Kalpa are a luxury. From there, we can see the mountain of Kinnaur Kailash (which we don't actually see until the next morning).

I go to the terrace to see the sunset. The sky is a beautiful hue of orange.

![kalpa](kalpa.avif)
*A few moments before the orange sky*

The Zostel turns pretty with lights after dusk.

![zostel](zostel.avif)
*Lights Camera Action*

There's a snooker table, and we have a few games along with snacks. It turns out Gyalli is a pro at pool.

![pool](pool.avif)
*A game of pool*

We have dinner. The group gathers as it's the last day, and we play the last mafia game. It's late, and I go to sleep at 0500 in the morning.
