# Formulas

I really like looking at the mathematical representations of any concepts. Here I collect few of the formulas relating to some of the topics:

The easiest way to move through this folder is to begin with `Basics`, where I keep the notation that tends to travel across subjects, and then step into a subject folder from there. Each subject starts with its own `README`, which sets up the local symbols and the small block of prerequisites that make the later pages readable.

From there the child pages get narrower and more specific. I try to keep them sparse: mostly headings and formulas, with only a small amount of local explanation when the math would otherwise be harder to parse. That keeps the collection closer to a notebook than a textbook.
